About Me

My name is Tomasz Wolak, I'm a front-end web and app developer.

I am a front-end developer with a passion for creating stunning, user friendly and responsive web pages and applications. I utilise my creativity and problem solving skills to help bring products to life. I stick to requirements, manage priorities, iterate and deliver on time. As a front end engineer I will translate your vision into a code and present you with a first-class product. I am able to explain my works in an easily understood way to help you understand what I am working on. I specialise in CSS, JavaScript, JQuery and React, but I am constantly looking to improve my knowledge, sharpen my skills and to learn new technologies and frameworks. Please check out my projects and have a look at what I am capable of producing.
